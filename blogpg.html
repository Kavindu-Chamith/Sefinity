Exploring Nature at Peradeniya Botanical Garden - Our First Software Engineering Batch Meetup

March 09 2024

This year, our Software Engineering batch of 2022/2023 kicked off with an unforgettable meetup at the stunning Peradeniya Botanical Garden. Set amidst lush greenery and vibrant flora, it was the perfect environment for us to reconnect, relax, and bond beyond the usual academic setting.

The day was filled with laughter, games, and team-building activities, helping us strengthen friendships and make new connections within our batch. Walking among the towering palms, sharing stories, and capturing moments on camera, we truly immersed ourselves in nature and each other’s company.

This beautiful day at the garden wasn’t just about the fun and games; it symbolized a new chapter, one marked by unity and the promise of many more memorable adventures together. As we left with a sense of warmth and connection, it was clear that this first meetup had set the tone for an incredible year ahead!

Reaching New Heights at Colombo Lotus Tower - Our Second Software Engineering Batch Meetup

July 29 2024

After the success of our first meetup, our Software Engineering batch of 2022/2023 took things to new heights—literally! On July 29, 2024, we gathered at the iconic Colombo Lotus Tower, Sri Lanka’s tallest structure, where we were treated to panoramic views of the city and ocean. The breathtaking scenery from the top brought a unique sense of awe and excitement that made this meetup truly unforgettable.

Throughout the day, we explored the tower’s levels, capturing countless group photos and sharing moments we’ll cherish for years. We even added a touch of friendly competition with some games, creating a lively and spirited atmosphere. Between playing games and sharing meals, each activity strengthened our bond, bringing us closer together as a batch. This day at the Lotus Tower was filled with fun, laughter, and incredible views, making it yet another memorable chapter in our journey together!

Here's to many more meetups and adventures that take our friendships—and experiences—to new heights!

A Nature-Inspired Finale – Our Third and Final Meetup at Peradeniya Botanical Garden

Nov 12 2024

November 12, 2024, marked a special day for our Software Engineering batch as we returned to the enchanting Peradeniya Botanical Garden for our third and final meetup. The garden held as much magic as it did during our first visit, and this time, it felt even more meaningful as we gathered to celebrate the friendships we’ve nurtured over these past months.

We spent the day revisiting our favorite spots, engaging in outdoor games that sparked everyone’s competitive spirit, and sharing countless laughs. A highlight of the day was our team scavenger hunt, an exciting and interactive challenge that brought out the best in everyone. Whether it was racing through the gardens in search of clues or cheering each other on, the hunt created memories that will stay with us long after our university days.

As the sun began to set, we gathered for a final group reflection, filled with gratitude for the moments we’ve shared and the friendships we’ve built through these meetups. It was a heartwarming end to our series of gatherings and a perfect farewell to an incredible year together. Here’s to the adventures yet to come and to friendships that will last a lifetime!

SEFINITY Logo – A Symbol of Innovation and Endless Possibilities

I’m excited to introduce the SEFINITY logo—a minimalist design crafted to represent the Software Engineering department’s new website, Sefinity. This logo embodies the essence of programming, knowledge, and the spirit of innovation that defines our department.

The SEFINITY logo was created to symbolize not only the technical core of software engineering but also the department's dedication to lifelong learning and open-ended growth. The clean lines and thoughtful design represent a vision where knowledge continually evolves, offering a limitless platform for students to learn, create, and innovate.

Here, you can discover the meaning behind the SEFINITY logo. Each element tells its own story.



With Sefinity, we aim to provide an evolving space that reflects the dynamic nature of software engineering, inspiring students to explore, expand, and excel. This logo is more than an image—it’s a statement of the department's commitment to nurturing a community where the pursuit of knowledge never ends.
